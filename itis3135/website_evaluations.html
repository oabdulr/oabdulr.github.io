Good and Bad Website Evaluations

Bad Site

This site falls heavily under the C.R.A.P design, its very messy, unaligned and everything is stuck together, I'd rate this site a 3/10. It has a charm to it that makes it unique and weirdly plesent at the same time

  • Contrast - The pages contrast isnt too awful, there are some moments when the images cover things completely.
  • Repetition - This page has almost no repetition everything is random and all over the place, the borders are random and the font and location are as well
  • Alignment - The worst offender of them all, nothing is in order and aligned
  • Proximity - Nothing has space inbetween, its all stuck together

Good Site

This site follows a majority of the C.R.A.P design concepts, it executes them pretty well and keeps retained user base.

  • Contrast - The pages contrast is clean and simple, everything is easy to reader and understand.
  • Repetition - The iconic blue of Amazon is used often as well as their way of sorting items in small cubicals.
  • Alignment - Everything is properly aligned and is plesent to the eyes.
  • Proximity - Items are grouped with small gaps together then seperated by large gaps which flows and looks good.
